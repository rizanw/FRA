[Giveaway] #NoPlasticStraw Selfie Challenge

12 October 2019 Riz

Get a limited edition of Terrennial’s bamboo straw set for free.


Hello, earth friends! As you may have read our posts regarding the usage of single-use plastics—particularly straws and some fun facts about alternative, eco-friendly straw materials—we are here to encourage you further spread the campaign of reducing the usage of single-use plastics. We dare you to take a selfie with the theme #NoPlasticStraw.

To participate is really simple, just follow the steps below:

  1. Take a selfie with theme #NoPlasticStraw. You are free to get as creative as possible!
  2. Post your selfie on any social media (Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, you name it)
  3. Tag your friends (minimum of 3) and Terrennial's social media.
  4. Include Terrennial’s blogsite and don’t forget to add the hashtags : #NoPlasticStraw #terrennial #earthfriends
  5. Share your post and get as many likes as you can!
5 most creative posts and 100 lucky participants will be chosen as the winner! This challenge will end on November 19, 2019 and the winners will be announced at Terrennial’s blogsite on November 21, 2019.

Sharing is caring!
