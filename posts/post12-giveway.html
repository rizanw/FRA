[Giveaway] #NoPlasticStraw Selfie Challenge

12 October 2019 Riz

Get a limited terrennial’s bamboo straw set for free.
Hello earth friends! We dare you to take selfie with the theme #NoPlasticStraw.

Participation is really simple, just follow the bellow steps:

  • Take a selfie with theme #NoPlasticStraw. Get as creative as you possible can.
  • Post your selfie on any social media (twitter, facebook, Instagram)
  • Tag your friends (minimum 3) and terrennial social media.
  • include terrennial’s blogsite and don’t forget to give hashtags : #NoPlasticStraw #terrenial #earthfriend
  • Share your post and get likes as much as possible.
  • Tag your friends (minimum 3) and terrennial social media.
5 Creative post will be chosen and 100 lucky participants. Challenge will ends November 19, 2019 and announces on terrennial’s blogsite at November 21, 2019.

Sharing is caring!
